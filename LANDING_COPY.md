## 1. Copywriting & Tone Guidelines
To resonate with technical builders, Credex uses a distinct copywriting framework:
1. **Developer-First Clarity (No Enterprise Fluff):** Avoid generic SaaS buzzwords like "synergy," "cutting-edge optimization," or "intelligent spend ecosystems." Use precise, direct engineering terms like "redundant IDE autocomplete," "prompt caching," and "5-seat billing minimum penalties."
2. **Mathematically Defensible:** Every cost-saving claim must map to verified vendor pricing plans (May 2026 updates). Never promise arbitrary "80% savings" without showing the exact tool downgrade or API configuration math.
3. **High-Urgency & Fear of Waste:** Highlight that AI subscription sprawl is silent cash burn. Frames waste as money that could be spent on computing power or extended runway.
4. **Zero-Trust Privacy:** Heavily emphasize that the audit is database-free and zero-leakage, respecting developer privacy.

## 3. The 5 Core FAQs (Landing Page Database)

### Q1: How does the Credex Spend Audit work?
> **Answer:** Credex scans your active AI engineering and SaaS tools (including Cursor, Copilot, ChatGPT, Claude, and direct LLM APIs). By running your active seat counts and monthly spending against our verified May 2026 pricing engine, we detect duplicate subscriptions (e.g., paying for both Cursor and Copilot autocomplete), identify hidden tier minimum billing penalties (like Claude Team's 5-seat minimum), and recommend technical caching optimizations that immediately shave 30%-50% off your active spend.

### Q2: What is the "Claude Team" seat penalty you mention?
> **Answer:** Many small startups upgrade to "Claude Team" thinking it's the natural progression from Claude Pro. However, Claude Team costs $30/user/mo and carries a strict **minimum of 5 seats ($150/mo minimum)**. If you have only 2 or 3 developers active, Anthropic still bills you for 5. Credex detects this and calculates the exact monthly cash savings of downgrading back to Claude Pro ($20/mo per seat) while retaining full capabilities.

### Q3: Is my company's financial or stack data secure?
> **Answer:** Absolutely. Credex is built on a "Zero-Leakage Architecture." We do not require active API database access or credentials for the initial spend evaluation. When you share a report card via our Base64 URL generator, the entire configuration is encoded directly inside the shareable string. The moment the link is loaded, it decodes solely within the viewer's browser memory—meaning your company name, email, and private stats are completely omitted and never stored on our servers.

### Q4: How do prompt caching optimizations save money?
> **Answer:** If your engineering stack relies on direct API calls to OpenAI or Anthropic exceeding $150/mo, input system prompts represent up to 80% of your total billing weight. By implementing prompt context caching, subsequent API requests reuse processed tokens, resulting in a 50% to 90% discount on input token pricing. Credex calculates your direct API spend and estimates a conservative 30% total bill reduction by utilizing caching.

### Q5: How do I qualify for the $100,000 in startup cloud credits?
> **Answer:** Major cloud providers (like AWS Activate and Google Cloud for Startups) alongside key AI suppliers (like Anthropic) offer high-value startup credits. Startups showing an active monthly AI tool and API spend of $\ge \$200$ qualify for these fast-track accelerator grants. Credex automatically evaluates your spend threshold and guides you on how to claim and secure these non-dilutive grants immediately upon completing the audit.
